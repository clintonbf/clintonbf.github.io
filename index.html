About me

I moved to Vancouver for work in Bioinformatics some years ago. I had a good mix of programming and biology in school and both disciplines interested me (and still do).

After a few years in that field, global economics conspired to make bioinformatics a difficult career to pursue in Vancouver. I was reluctant to leave the life I had made here and so took a position at my martial arts school, and became one of the 20th century's first professional swordfighters (don't fact check that). Having done that for a few years I wanted a change, which is what has brought me to the CST program.
